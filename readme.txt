The WooCommerce Zoho CRM Integration sync the products, the customer to contact and the orders to sales orders in Zoho CRM.

== Description ==

The WooCommerce Zoho CRM Integration sync the products, the customer to contact and the orders to sales orders in Zoho CRM. and the customer automatically sync to zoho CRM account when 
order is placed in WooCommerce.

== Installation ==

* Click "Plugins" > "Add New in the WordPress admin menu.
* Search for "WooCommerce Zoho CRM Integration".
* Click "Install Now".
* Click "Activate Plugin".

* Alternatively, you can manually upload the plugin to your wp-content/plugins directory.


== Create Zoho CRM Account ==
First you need create Zoho CRM Account in https://crm.zoho.in or https://crm.zoho.com or https://crm.zoho.eu or https://crm.zoho.com.cn any one.
